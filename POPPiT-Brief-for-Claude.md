# POPPiT Website — Creative Brief for Review

Please review this brief and give holistic feedback on the website design, brand alignment, copy, section order, and anything that could be improved. Be direct and specific.

---

## What is POPPiT?

POPPiT is a **protein candy brand** by Roadies Nutrition LLC. It's real gummy candy — bouncy, chewy, candy-store texture — that also delivers functional nutrition:

- **10g protein** per serving (clean peptide protein)
- **5g fiber**
- **2g sugar** (sweetened with allulose + natural flavors)
- **~80 calories**

**Tagline:** Savor the Moment  
**Voice:** Bold, irreverent, candy-forward. Talks to people who want to eat candy without the guilt. Tone references: "Unhinged.", "Absolutely feral.", "Help." — fun, self-aware, not overly gym-bro.

**Target audience:** Adults 18–35 who snack, gym or not, and are tired of protein bars that taste like chalk.

---

## Brand Identity

**Colors:**
- Background: near-black `#0a0a0a`
- Primary CTA: deep red `#a81c1c`
- Gold (headings/accents): `#e8b820` / `#f5d44b` / `#c49518`
- Flavor accents: Electric blue `#00d4ff` / Magenta `#ff2d7b` / Lime `#b8ff00`

**Fonts:**
- **Boogaloo** — all major headings (fun, rounded, candy energy)
- **Space Mono** — nav, labels, CTAs (techy, precise contrast)
- **DM Sans** — body copy (clean, readable)

**Logo:** Real POPPiT wordmark image in the nav. Gold gradient with red drop shadow on the hero text version.
